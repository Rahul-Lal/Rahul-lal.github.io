What is Emotional Intelligence

Emotional Intelligence or Emotional Quotient (EQ) is when someone is able to know, control and express their emotions, and those around them.
Reference

Different from Intelligence Quotient (IQ)

  • IQ - Used to understanding information and apply practically using objects and data
    • E.G. Learning a new skill through a tutorial
  • EQ - Used to understanding information using our own or other's emotions
    • E.G. Seeing a situation if you were that person in that situation

Why is it important

Emotional Intelligence is vital to certain aspects of people and how they handle them such as:

  • Their relationships
  • Their leadership
  • Their perspective
