Trying out the Pomodoro technique





The Pomodoro technique

A method to manage time, focus, and productivity. What you do is focus on a task to be done usually for 25 minutes, afterwards have a short 5 minute break.

My experience using Porodomo

I use a application on my phone as opppose to a tab on my browser, as for a tab I constantly keep checking the timer which counteracts the technique. While focusing on my work, the ticking of the app reminds me that I being timed. I set out one task and using the timer, I perform the Pomodoro as many times I can till I finish the task.

After that, take a short 5 minute break or a long 15 minute break, sometimes I just keep continue working within those breaks.

After finishing my first task, I realized how focused I was and how much my productivity has increased. The Pomodoro technique, I gonna keep using this.

One thing I find ironic is that Pomodoro is the italian word for tomato, and I don't like tomatoes. Just putting it out there!
